About Us

Here at Pam's Salmon Cookies, we like to keep things a little weird. Whether you're a local or tourist, we think you'll agree that being unique is what makes Seattlites, well... unique! So if you want a taste of some totally authentic, delicious Seattle kitsch, come on down to one of our many locations. Make sure to stop by in the winter for our pumpkin spice roe jam! We're open every day from 6am to 8pm.
